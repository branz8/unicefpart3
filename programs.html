Our Programs

UNICEF works worldwide to improve the lives of children and their families by providing access to healthcare, education, protection, and emergency relief. Here are some of the programs that help us make a difference.

Education for Every Child

Our education programs ensure that children everywhere have the opportunity to learn and reach their full potential, even in the most challenging conditions.

Healthcare and Nutrition

UNICEF provides life-saving healthcare and nutrition services to children in need, reducing malnutrition and promoting child survival and development.

Emergency Relief

In times of crisis, UNICEF is on the ground, providing children and families with immediate relief and long-term support to rebuild their lives.
