Your generous donation will help children around the world. Every bit counts in making a difference to a child's life, be it providing clean water, education, or healthcare.

Your Impact

With your donations, UNICEF is able to provide clean drinking water, food, healthcare, education, and emergency relief to children in over 190 countries.

  • $50 can provide clean water to a village for one month.
  • $100 can provide essential healthcare supplies to a clinic.
  • $250 can help fund education programs for children.
