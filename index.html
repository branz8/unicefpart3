What We Do

Education for All

We ensure that children worldwide have access to quality education regardless of their circumstances. Over 500,000 children have been enrolled in schools through our initiatives.

Healthcare Services

We provide life-saving healthcare solutions, including vaccinations, nutrition, and clean water access. This year alone, we reached 2 million children in need of medical care.

Emergency Relief

Our emergency relief programs assist children affected by natural disasters, wars, and conflicts. UNICEF has responded to over 200 emergencies globally in the last decade.

Success Stories

Bringing Education to Refugees

UNICEF built schools in refugee camps, giving hope and opportunities to thousands of displaced children. Over 80,000 children have received education in conflict zones through these programs.

Ending Hunger in Africa

We distributed food and nutritional supplies to millions of children and families during the drought crisis in Africa. Our food distribution efforts have reduced malnutrition rates by 25%.

Latest News

UNICEF Expands Vaccination Programs

UNICEF is launching a global vaccination initiative to ensure every child has access to life-saving vaccines.

In partnership with WHO, UNICEF is expanding its efforts to deliver vaccines to remote regions. This initiative will save millions of lives by providing access to essential vaccines for diseases such as polio, measles, and more. Our goal is to reach 10 million children by the end of the year.

Education Programs in Conflict Zones

UNICEF's educational programs aim to reach children affected by conflicts worldwide.

Working in regions like Syria, South Sudan, and Yemen, UNICEF is focused on building schools, providing materials, and training teachers to help restore education in war-torn areas. Over 300 schools have been rebuilt, and 120,000 children are back in classrooms.
